# A Visit to New Possibilities

*Published on December 25, 2019 • Travel & Reflection*

It’s not easy to just forget and move on in life for people like us. Forgetting here refers to those instances which made some differences in the daily routine, the thoughts, the approaches, the plans, and most importantly, the Life. I wouldn’t be just considering myself as the writer of this blog, but maybe I am considering some of us with the exact same thought.

When such a situation occurs, we come to choose the "right wrong" without even considering the fact whether it is right or wrong. I wanted some change in my life to just restart the whole thing; I wished for some reset button that could just refresh my memories only with the good ones and not the bad ones anymore. But that’s quite impossible for now.

One of my friends asked me to move to a place, a cold one instead, just for fun and a changeover. I accepted the invitation without considering any fact. "Fun" was what I needed.

Stepping into a different environment, seeing how others live, and letting the cold air clear my thoughts brought back a sense of perspective. Reset buttons don't exist in hardware, but they do exist in how we decide to look at our surroundings. When you change your location, you give your mind the permission to think differently.
